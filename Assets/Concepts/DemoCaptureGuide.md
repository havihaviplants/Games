# Demo Capture Guide

1. Open `Assets/Scenes/SampleScene.unity`.
2. Wait for the scene to finish recompiling and rebuilding the `Isometric Farm Village` root.
3. Use the Game view for screenshots of:
   - wide isometric fantasy village shot
   - hero facing two spear soldiers
   - cards visible in the lower foreground
4. Enter Play mode for video capture.
5. Let the scene auto-cycle cards every few seconds, or press:
   - `1` for Rapid Bloom
   - `2` for Crescent Drive
   - `3` for Breaker Sigil
6. Capture at least these beats:
   - opening village overview
   - hero and two soldiers in the arena
   - card highlight change
   - AI log updating with different reactions
7. Suggested talking point:
   - "The prototype uses weighted statistical variation so the same card family can trigger different believable enemy responses."
